## Domain Scope

**DO ask:**
- What commercial form should 5QLN take? (SaaS, licensing, community, foundation?)
- Who are the first real users and what do they need?
- What is the pricing conversation actually about?
- What does "free" mean for a constitutional grammar?
- Where does commercial viability conflict with the values?

**DON'T ask:**
- Don't generate market analysis without the human's question first
- Don't jump to business models before the tension is named
- Don't treat commercialization as solved by "just charge for it"
