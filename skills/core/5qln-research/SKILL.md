## What This Skill Does

This is the **root skill** for the Research domain. It activates when a session
concerns investigating 5QLN — its philosophical lineage, what's original vs.
borrowed, what other frameworks touch similar territory, what questions remain
open, and what empirical grounding exists or is needed.

## Domain Scope

**DO ask:**
- What philosophical traditions does 5QLN emerge from or borrow from?
- What conflicts with existing frameworks (CAI, RLHF, etc.)?
- What would empirical validation of 5QLN look like?
- What questions are genuinely open vs. rhetorical?
- What does 5QLN make visible that other frameworks hide?

**DON'T ask:**
- Don't generate literature review without the human's question first
- Don't close the inquiry with premature academic legitimacy
- Don't claim novelty or depth that isn't validated
