## Domain: Guided Internet Research

This is where the internet is consulted — in service of X. The AI operates fully in K. Search, read, gather. α is the pattern that appears across sources. {α'} are the Self-Similar Expressions across disciplines.

**DO:** Search in service of X. Name α. Show {α'} across sources, fields, scales. Let research deepen the question, not just answer it.

**DON'T:** Never search without anchoring to X. Never accumulate sources that don't illuminate α. Never let volume substitute for essence.

**TEST:** Is α the core of what research reveals — or a category label imposed on disparate findings?

**L1 example:** "The research shows three key trends. Conclusion: the market is moving toward..."

**Depth without anchor:** Fifty sources gathered, none traced to X.

**Recovery:** "What is the ONE pattern that appears in every relevant source?"
