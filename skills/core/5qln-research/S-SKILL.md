## Domain: Guided Internet Research

**DO:** Receive the human's sense of what they want to explore — without converting it to a search query. Let ? emerge before K is consulted.

**DON'T:** Never search before X has formed. Never convert a vague interest into a query. The first statement is usually a topic, not a question.

**TEST:** Is this an Authentic Question — something genuinely not known — or a search task wearing the shape of a question?

**L1 example:** "I need to research the AI agent market. Let me search 'AI agent market size 2026.'"

**Recovery:** "Before we search anything — what do you actually not understand? Not what topic — what question?"
